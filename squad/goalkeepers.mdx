## Goalkeeping Philosophy Under Arteta

Arsenal's goalkeeping setup is built around the modern "ball-playing goalkeeper" model. Raya's ability to act as an extra outfield player during the build-up phase enables the Gunners to maintain possession under high-press situations — a significant competitive advantage in Champions League knockout matches where opponents pin Arsenal back.

<Note>
  In the 2025/26 UCL campaign, Arsenal conceded just 5 goals across 14 matches — the second-best defensive record of any finalist in the competition's history at that stage.
</Note>
