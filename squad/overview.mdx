## 2025/26 First-Team Squad

Arsenal's 2025/26 squad represents the most complete group of players assembled under Mikel Arteta. New sporting director Andrea Berta oversaw a summer spend of nearly £200 million, with six first-team additions — most notably striker Viktor Gyökeres, the long-sought answer to Arsenal's centre-forward question.

<Info>
  Under-21 players (born on or after 1 January 2004) are eligible for the Premier League without occupying a place on the 25-man registered squad list. Ethan Nwaneri, Myles Lewis-Skelly, and others benefit from this rule.
</Info>

## Key Renewals & Extensions

Several core players signed long-term extensions heading into the season, signalling the club's intent to hold together a title-winning core:

- **Bukayo Saka** — 4-year extension until 2030, £300,000/week
- **William Saliba** — 5-year extension until 2030, £250,000/week
- **Gabriel Magalhães** — 4-year extension until 2029, £200,000/week
