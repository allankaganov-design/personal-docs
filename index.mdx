## Welcome to the Arsenal FC Intelligence Hub

This hub is your single source of truth for everything Arsenal — from the depth of the 2025/26 squad to the financial engine powering Mikel Arteta's project, a century of history at the club, and the tactical blueprint for capturing the one trophy that has eluded the Gunners: the UEFA Champions League.

Arsenal reached the Champions League final for only the second time in their history on 30 May 2026, falling to Paris Saint-Germain on penalties in Budapest. The journey, the heartbreak, and the lessons learned are all documented here — alongside the roadmap for 2026/27.
